Invoice-IQ ("the App") is an accounts payable automation tool operated by {{ company_name }} ("we", "us", "our") for its own internal use. This policy explains what data the App processes and how it is handled.

1. What data we process

  • Invoice documents — PDF/image files received by email or uploaded manually, and the structured data extracted from them (supplier name, invoice number, dates, amounts, line items).
  • Accounting data — vendor records, purchase orders, and bill/invoice records read from and written to the connected accounting system (QuickBooks Online, Xero, or Sage 50).
  • Email metadata — sender, subject, and attachment names from the monitored mailbox, used solely to identify and retrieve invoice attachments.
  • User account data — name, email address, and a securely hashed password for each person who logs into the App.

2. How we use this data

Data is used exclusively to operate the App's core function: capturing supplier invoices, extracting their content using AI, matching them against purchase orders, routing them for approval, and posting approved invoices to the connected accounting system. We do not sell, rent, or use this data for advertising.

3. Third-party processors

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — invoice document images are sent to Anthropic's API to extract structured data. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs that processing.
  • Microsoft 365 / Microsoft Graph API — used to read the monitored mailbox and retrieve invoice attachments.
  • QuickBooks Online / Xero / Sage 50 — the connected accounting system, used to read purchase orders and vendors and to post approved bills.

4. Data storage and retention

Invoice files and extracted data are stored in a private database and file store operated on our behalf by our hosting provider. Data is retained for as long as the App is in active use for our business records, and is deleted on request or on account closure.

5. Security

Access to the App requires an individual login. Passwords are stored as one-way cryptographic hashes, never in plain text. Connections to the App and to all third-party services are encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

6. Your rights

As this App is operated for internal company use, individuals whose data is processed (e.g. named contacts on supplier invoices) may contact us using the details below to ask what data we hold about them or to request its deletion, subject to our legitimate business record-keeping obligations.
