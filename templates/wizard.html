Connect your invoice mailbox

InvoiceIQ monitors an O365 mailbox for incoming invoice emails. You'll need an Azure App Registration with Mail.Read and Mail.ReadWrite permissions.

Add your Claude AI key

InvoiceIQ uses Claude to read and extract data from invoice PDFs and images. Get your API key from console.anthropic.com — a free tier is available to get started.

Your key is stored locally and never transmitted anywhere except directly to Anthropic.
